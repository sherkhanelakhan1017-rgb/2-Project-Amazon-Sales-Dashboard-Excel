# Amazon Sales & Profit Dashboard (2023–2024)

## 📊 Project Overview
This project presents an interactive Excel dashboard analyzing Amazon sales and profit performance for 2023 and 2024.

The dashboard provides insights into:
- Sales performance by city and state
- Monthly sales trends
- Product-wise unit sales
- Regional sales distribution
- KPI tracking and profit analysis

---

## 🛠 Tools & Features Used
- Microsoft Excel
- Pivot Tables
- Pivot Charts
- KPI Cards
- Slicers
- Map Charts
- Doughnut Charts
- Business Reporting

---

## 📈 Key Insights
- West region contributed the highest overall sales.
- Nestle Smarties Pop-Up recorded the highest units sold.
- 2024 sales outperformed 2023 during peak months.
- Significant revenue growth observed in Nov–Dec.
- Delhi emerged as the top-performing city by sales.
